---
description: Verify that email notifications are working correctly
---

1. Navigate to the Settings page.
2. Locate the "Email Test" section.
3. Enter a valid email address in the input field.
4. Click the "Test Email" button.
5. Verify that a success toast message appears.
6. Check the inbox of the entered email address for a "Test Email from Mist Umbra".
7. (Optional) Check the database `email_logs` table to confirm the email was logged with status 'sent'.
